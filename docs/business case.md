## Business Case: Strategic Contact Extraction

This system was developed to support the creation of the **2026 Marketing & Sales Strategy** by providing a clear view of who within client organizations communicates with us and in what capacity.

---

## Problem Statement

Client communication often involves individuals from multiple departments, including:
* Marketing
* Purchasing
* Engineering
* HR
* Operations
* Product Management

However, this critical intelligence is **locked inside inconsistent, multilingual email signatures**.

**Manual analysis of thousands of emails is too slow and error-prone** to support timely strategic planning and account structure identification.

---

## Objective

The primary objective was to **automatically extract and consolidate** key contact data from **11,352 incoming emails** distributed across multiple mailbox folders.

**Target Data Fields:**
* Names
* Job titles
* Departments
* Organizational roles

**Strategic Goals:**
This allows the organization to:
* Identify key stakeholders
* Build communication maps
* Understand account structures
* Support data-driven decision-making

---

## Outcome

The project successfully delivered a clean, consistent dataset that is suitable for downstream business intelligence workflows.

* **300 unique contact profiles extracted**
* **Clean, consistent dataset suitable for:**
    * CRM enrichment
    * Stakeholder mapping
    * Strategy workshops
    * Cross-functional alignment
* **Reduced multi-week manual workload to minutes**
* Established repeatable annual process for contact intelligence gathering.

---

## Business Value

The system enabled the marketing and leadership teams to achieve critical strategic clarity:

| Strategic Benefit | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Account Insight** | Identify engagement points within major accounts and which departments contact us most. |
| **Communication Planning** | Map decision-maker chains to improve targeted communication strategies. |
| **Data-Driven Strategy** | Prepare data-backed documentation for the 2026 planning cycle. |
| **Efficiency** | Increased clarity, efficiency, and insight for strategic planning. |
